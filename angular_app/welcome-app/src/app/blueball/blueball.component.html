Welcome to the Blue Ball Game

Objective :

    There is one blue ball in the list of the given balls. Your goal is to find out which ball is the blue ball.

Mechanics of the game :

  • Pick a ball from the given list of balls
  • If the Selected ball turns Red it means the blue ball is not before the selected ball.
  • If the selected ball turns Green it means the blue ball is not after the selected ball.
